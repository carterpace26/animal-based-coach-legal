Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Your subscription is billed by Apple, not by us, so it is cancelled through Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Animal Based Coach → Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the end of the period you have already paid for.

If you cancel during the 14-day free trial, you are not charged at all. Cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends — Apple bills automatically after that.

I paid, but the app still shows the paywall.

Open the app and go to Profile → Restore Purchases. This asks Apple directly what you own and re-links it to your account. It is the fix for a new phone, a reinstall, or a purchase that did not register.

If that does not work, email us. Do not buy a second subscription — we would rather fix it than take your money twice.

Why does the app ask for Apple Health?

So it can prove whether a change to your diet actually moved anything. It reads four things — heart rate variability, resting heart rate, sleep and steps — and it only ever reads. Nothing is written back to Health, ever.

The app works without it. You will still get your plan, your check-ins, your coach and your weekly verdict. What you lose is the passive proof: without a wearable, the app will not show you HRV or resting-heart-rate numbers, because it refuses to invent them.

You can grant or revoke access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Animal Based Coach.

How do I sign in? There is no password.

There isn't one, on purpose. You sign in with Sign in with Apple — one tap, no password to remember and none for us to leak. It works on any iPhone signed in to an Apple Account.

How do I delete my account and everything in it?

In the app: Profile → Delete Account. This is permanent and immediate. It erases your profile, your check-ins, your plans, your bloodwork and your conversations with the coach from our servers. We do not keep a copy.

Deleting your account does not cancel your Apple subscription — cancel that separately in Settings (see above), or Apple will keep billing you for an app you no longer have an account on.

Is the app medical advice?

No. Animal Based Coach is a nutrition and habit-tracking tool. It is not a doctor, it does not diagnose, and it does not treat anything. If you are managing a medical condition, are pregnant, or take prescription medication, talk to your clinician before changing how you eat. If you think you are having a medical emergency, call your local emergency number.

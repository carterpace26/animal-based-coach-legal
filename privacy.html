Animal Based Coach

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

This policy explains what Animal Based Coach collects, why, and what we do with it. It is written to be read, not to be survived.

The short version

  • Your data lives on your phone. There is no account database. We are not storing your profile, your check-ins, your weight, or your chat history on a server.
  • To answer you, the app sends the information the coach needs to an AI model provider. That is the only place your data goes.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. There are no ad networks, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers in this app.
  • You can delete everything, permanently, from inside the app: Profile → Delete my account and all data.

What we collect, and where it lives

Everything you enter is stored locally on your device. That includes:

  • Your name and email
  • Your body stats (sex, age, height, weight, goal weight)
  • Your goals, training schedule, food preferences, budget and allergies
  • Any health conditions, medications and blood test results you choose to enter
  • Your daily check-ins (how you feel, energy, digestion, sleep, weight)
  • Your meal plans, experiments and findings
  • Your conversations with the coach

We do not have a copy. If you delete the app or delete your account in-app, that data is gone — including from us, because we never had it.

Health information

Some of what you can enter is sensitive: blood markers, medications, health conditions, an eating-disorder history. You choose whether to provide any of it. The app works without it; the coaching is simply less specific. It is stored on your device with everything else and is only ever sent where described below.

Where your data is sent

To answer a question, build your meal plan, or read your week, the app sends the relevant parts of the above to an AI model provider (currently Google Gemini) through our backend.

  • Our backend is a relay. It passes the request to the model and passes the answer back. It does not keep your data after the request completes.
  • The model provider processes the request under their own terms. They do not use data sent through the paid API to train their models.
  • Requests are sent over HTTPS.

If you use the restaurant finder, the app sends the city or approximate location you provide, so the coach can suggest places near you. Your precise location is never stored.

If you scan a blood test report, the photo is sent to the model to read the numbers out of it, and is not retained afterwards.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in this app.
  • We do not read your Apple Health data. The app has no Health integration.

Payments

Subscriptions are handled by Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple processes the payment. We never see your card number. We receive only whether your subscription is active.

Your rights

  • Delete everything: Profile → Delete my account and all data. It is immediate and permanent.
  • Delete your conversations only: Profile → Delete all coach conversations.
  • Access your data: it is on your device, in the app. There is no hidden copy to request.
  • If you are in the EU/UK (GDPR) or California (CCPA/CPRA), the rights those laws give you — access, correction, deletion, and not having your data sold — are satisfied by the above, because we do not hold your data and we do not sell it.

Children

Animal Based Coach is not intended for anyone under 13, and the app does not accept an age below 13. If you believe a child has used the app, delete the account in-app; that removes everything.

This is not medical advice

Animal Based Coach provides nutrition and lifestyle coaching. It is not a medical device, and it does not diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease. Nothing in the app is a substitute for advice from a qualified healthcare professional. Do not use it to make medical decisions, and speak to your doctor before making significant changes to your diet — particularly if you are pregnant, have a medical condition, or take medication.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the date at the top and notify you in the app.
